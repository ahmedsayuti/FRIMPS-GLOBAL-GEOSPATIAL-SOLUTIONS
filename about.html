Our Mission

At FGGS, we harness cutting-edge geospatial technology to address complex environmental and developmental challenges across Ghana and West Africa. Our mission is to transform raw spatial data into actionable intelligence that informs policy, drives sustainable business decisions, and empowers communities.

Through our expertise in GIS, remote sensing, and environmental data analysis, we provide solutions that contribute to sustainable urban development, climate resilience, natural resource management, and improved agricultural productivity throughout the region.

Our Journey

Milestones in FGGS's growth and impact

2013

Foundation Established

FGGS founded by Dr. Bernard Fosu Frimpong to provide specialized geospatial consulting services in Accra, Ghana.

2015

First Major Government Contract

Awarded a significant project with Ghana's Ministry of Food and Agriculture for nationwide crop monitoring.

2018

Expansion to Regional Markets

Began operations in Nigeria and Côte d'Ivoire, establishing FGGS as a regional leader in geospatial solutions.

2021

AI Integration Initiative

Incorporated machine learning and artificial intelligence into our geospatial analysis capabilities.

2023

Geospatial Excellence Award

Received national recognition for innovative approaches in environmental monitoring using satellite technology.
