Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of geospatial services do you offer?

    We offer a comprehensive range of geospatial services including GIS mapping and analysis, remote sensing, environmental data solutions, and geospatial consulting. Our services cater to various industries such as agriculture, urban planning, environmental management, and resource exploration.

  • How long does a typical project take to complete?

    Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. Simple mapping projects might take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive geospatial analysis projects can take several months. After discussing your specific needs, we'll provide a detailed timeline during the proposal stage.

  • Do you work with international clients?

    Yes, we serve clients worldwide. While we're based in Ghana, we have successfully completed projects for clients across Africa, Europe, and North America. We utilize digital collaboration tools to ensure seamless communication regardless of location.

  • What file formats do you work with?

    We work with all standard geospatial file formats including Shapefile, GeoJSON, KML, GeoTIFF, and CAD files. We're also experienced with data from various satellite platforms and can work with proprietary formats from ESRI, Google, and other providers.

  • Can you help with custom GIS application development?

    Absolutely! We specialize in developing custom GIS applications tailored to your specific needs. Our solutions range from web-based mapping applications to mobile data collection tools and enterprise GIS systems integrated with your existing infrastructure.

  • What is your pricing structure?

    We offer both project-based pricing and retainer models depending on your needs. After understanding your requirements, we'll provide a detailed quote outlining costs. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

  • Do you provide training services?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs on various geospatial technologies. Our training can be tailored to different skill levels, from beginner to advanced users, and can cover specific software platforms or analytical techniques.

  • How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?

    We take data security seriously. All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We're happy to sign NDAs and can arrange secure data transfer methods. Our team follows strict confidentiality protocols to protect your information.
