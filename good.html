About FGGS

FRIMPS Global Geospatial Solutions (FGGS) is a registered geospatial technology and consultancy firm based in Accra, Ghana, under the leadership of Dr. Bernard Fosu Frimpong. With over a decade of experience in the geospatial industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for organizations across Africa and beyond.

We specialize in transforming complex spatial data into actionable intelligence for decision-makers in various sectors including agriculture, urban planning, environmental management, and resource exploration. Our team of experts combines technical expertise with deep domain knowledge to deliver solutions that address real-world challenges.

At FGGS, we are committed to innovation, quality, and sustainability. We continuously invest in research and development to stay at the forefront of geospatial technology. Our mission is to empower organizations with spatial intelligence that drives growth, efficiency, and sustainable development.
