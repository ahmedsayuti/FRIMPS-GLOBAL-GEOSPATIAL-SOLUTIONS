About FGGS

FRIMPS Global Geospatial Solutions (FGGS) is a registered geospatial technology and consultancy firm based in Accra, Ghana, under the leadership of Dr. Bernard Fosu Frimpong.

We specialize in transforming complex spatial data into actionable intelligence for decision-makers in various sectors including agriculture, urban planning, environmental management, and resource exploration.
